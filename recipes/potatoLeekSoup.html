Winter Potato and Leek Soup

Winter leek and potato soup is a great-tasting, hearty, and satisfying soup that's perfect for a lunch or to start an otherwise light dinner. Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 45 mins Total Time: 1 hr 5 mins

Procedure
  1. Melt butter in a pot over medium heat. Add leeks and onion; cook and stir until tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add potatoes and chicken broth; simmer until potatoes are tender, 20 to 25 minutes.
  3. Stir in half-and-half; simmer until slightly thickened, about 15 minutes more.
  4. Stir in Cheddar cheese, parsley, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
